Chat Analyze Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Chat Analyze Pro ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Chat Analyze Pro mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the Service collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data Transmission — Nothing Leaves Your Device

We NEVER transmit, upload or share any chat content, personal data or device identifiers to any cloud server, third-party server, analytics SDK or advertising network. All parsing and analysis runs entirely on your device locally. No outbound network calls are made for any chat-related data.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

When you import a chat export file, the Service stores the parsed chat report locally on your device so you can review it later in My Reports. Each locally stored report may include: Chat participant nicknames · Message timestamps and dates · Message text content · Derived statistics (participation ratio, sentiment scores, etc.) · The file name you selected.

We keep locally up to the 10 most recent reports. Oldest reports are automatically removed when you import additional chats beyond that limit.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect: Device identifiers (IMEI, IDFA, OAID, MAC address, IP address) · Location data, camera roll, microphone, contacts, calendar or Bluetooth data · Crash logs, analytics events or telemetry · Any data from your other apps or social media accounts.

4. How to Delete Your Data

All locally stored reports remain on your device until you delete them. You can delete them at any time by: Opening My Reports → long-pressing a card → tapping Delete · Or uninstalling the app.

You can also clear all local reports at once from Settings → Clear All Data.

5. Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

The Service does not display advertisements and does not integrate any third-party advertising SDK, analytics SDK or attribution SDK.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent or share your personal information or chat content with any third parties for commercial or other purposes.

7. Data Security

Local data on your device is protected by the operating system sandbox (Android Application Sandbox / iOS App Sandbox), which prevents other apps from reading it. We do not password-encrypt reports ourselves, because the original chat text is never transmitted and remains under your exclusive physical control of the device.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at persons under 14 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
